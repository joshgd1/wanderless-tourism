# GAP: No Pricing Elasticity Analysis

## Gap

The pitch deck proposes 15-18% commission as "industry standard" without analyzing whether this is the **optimal rate** for WanderLess's specific context.

## What We Don't Know

1. Price elasticity: How does guide behavior change at different commission rates?
2. Tourist price sensitivity: Does commission get built into price tourists pay?
3. Competitive equilibrium: What rate would maximize total platform volume?
4. Guide tier pricing: Does the $14.99 premium tool fee stack合理?

## The Risk

Setting commission too high → guides migrate to direct booking (20-30% expected anyway)
Setting commission too low → leaving money on table, not optimizing for profitability

## Missing Analysis

- No mention of commission rate optimization
- No A/B test plan for different rates
- No analysis of how Airbnb (14.2%) vs Klook vs Viator rates compare
- No model of guide economics at different commission levels

## What Needs to Happen

1. Build guide economics model at different commission rates
2. Survey guides on willingness to pay at different rates
3. Design gradual commission increase as guide value increases
4. Consider: Is 15-18% the profit-maximizing rate or just the industry convention?
